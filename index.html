Lesson 2.1 Anatomy of the Candle

Decoding the "Battle Report"

Think of a single candlestick as a time-compressed summary of a war. The body shows who conquered the territory, while the wicks show where the enemy (counter-party liquidity) was too strong to overcome.

The Flawless Logic of Wicks:

Lower Wicks: These are not just lines; they are "V-Shape" rejections. They prove that the algorithm reached down to find buy orders (liquidity) and instantly moved away.
Upper Wicks: These prove that the "Ceiling" of orders was too heavy for price to break. We call this "Supply Overload."

Lesson 2.2 Trend Identification

The Pulse: HH, HL, LH, LL

Market structure is the ONLY thing that tells you which way to trade. If the structure is Bullish, you only look for Buy entries at the "Discount" (Higher Low). If it's Bearish, you only Sell at the "Premium" (Lower High).

UPTREND HH + HL

The market is "stepping" up. Every pull back is simply the algorithm re-loading its buy orders.

GOLDEN RULE: Do not Sell an Uptrend. Wait for the HL.

DOWNTREND LH + LL

The market is "cascading" down. Gravity is fueled by supply and fear. Bounces are fake.

GOLDEN RULE: Do not Buy a Downtrend. Wait for the LH.
