Level 1: The Microscope

Candle Storytelling

At Skillforge, we don't care if a candle is green or red. We care about Pressure. A candle is simply a visual representation of a battle between the Algorithm and the Available Orders.

"The Body tells you where the price survived. The Wick tells you where the price was murdered."

The Boss (Momentum)

"Maximum Intent"

The Algorithm has a target and is moving fast.

The Reject (Wick)

"Price Rejection"

The price entered a zone it wasn't allowed to stay in.

The Nap (Doji)

"Indecision"

The market is 'reloading'. Do not trade until a Boss appears.
